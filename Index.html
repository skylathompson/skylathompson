What is a pet?

A pet is a companion animal which is an animal that lives with you in your home like a dog or cat.

Companion animals are a result of domestication. They live with us because they fulfill our needs as well.

They can provide us comfort, friendship and responsibility.

Most Common Pets In Australia

  • Dogs: 40% of Households
  • Cats: 27% of Households
  • Fish: 11% of Households

funfact!

Cats have better memories than dogs. Tests conducted by the University of Michigan concluded that while a dog’s memory lasts no more than 5 minutes, a cat’s can last as long as 16 hours—exceeding even that of monkeys and orangutans.
